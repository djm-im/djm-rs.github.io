Abou IT Konekt

ITkonekt is a unique event in IT world. On one place gathers the biggest IT companies, whole gaming community, IT experts, students of technical and IT sphere, as well as junior IT people, experienced Seniors and managers of big IT companies.

 

ITkonekt is divided into three parts:

ITjobfair

All talented IT experts in one place. Find perfect profile of IT expert for your job, internship or sproject.

ITconference

Find out what are future trends on tech market, in the biggest IT companies etc. ITkonekt will bring together the biggest experts from leading IT companies in the world.

GameSpace

Unique opportunity to try newest games on the market, code parts of games, have fun etc.
